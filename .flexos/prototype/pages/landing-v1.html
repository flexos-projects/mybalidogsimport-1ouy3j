The Challenge of International Adoption

Rescuing a dog from Bali is a noble act, but the journey to a forever home abroad is filled with complex regulations, mountains of paperwork, and logistical hurdles that can cause delays and stress for everyone involved.

Our Solution

MyBaliDogsImport is a dedicated platform that simplifies the entire process. We provide rescue organizations with the tools to manage dog profiles, automate compliance documents, and track each dog's journey in real-time, ensuring a smoother, more transparent path to adoption.

A Platform Built for Rescue

We offer a hyper-focused, end-to-end solution specifically tailored for the unique challenges of Bali-to-international dog imports.

Compliance Automation

Automatically generate required health certificates and import permits based on destination country rules with interactive checklists.

Journey Tracking

Provide a visual timeline and real-time status updates for each dog's journey, accessible to staff, adopters, and volunteers.

Stakeholder Hub

Facilitate communication and matching between rescue admins, international adopters, and volunteer flight companions.
